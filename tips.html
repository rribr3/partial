Roll, Don't Fold

Rolling your clothes instead of folding them has several advantages:

  • Saves up to 30% more space in your luggage
  • Reduces wrinkles and creases in your clothing
  • Makes it easier to see all items at a glance
  • Allows for better organization with packing cubes
Pro Tip: Roll similar items together (like shirts with undershirts) to create outfit bundles.
Use Packing Cubes

Packing cubes are a game-changer for organized travel:

  • Compress clothing to save valuable suitcase space
  • Keep similar items together (undergarments, tops, bottoms)
  • Separate clean and dirty clothes during your trip
  • Make unpacking at your destination a breeze
Pro Tip: Use different colored cubes for each family member or category of items.
Pack by Outfit

Packing by outfit helps eliminate unnecessary items:

  • Plan complete outfits for each day/activity
  • Choose versatile pieces that can mix and match
  • Limit shoes to 2-3 pairs that work with multiple outfits
  • Consider doing laundry on longer trips
Pro Tip: Take photos of your planned outfits before packing to visualize combinations.
